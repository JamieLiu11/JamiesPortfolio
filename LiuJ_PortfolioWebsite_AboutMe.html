{Intro}

My name is Jamie Liu and I am a 3D artist and animator from Toronto, Ontario, Canada. I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in digital arts and sciences and would hope to live out my dream as a professional film and television character animator.
I fell in love with my first animated film in the late 1990's when my dad brought home a VHS tape of the 1995 Disney animated film, "Pocahontas". Since then I've watched the field of animation grow into the art and technology it is today.
Naturally, I fell in love with everything 3D animation had to offer film. And that's how I ended up here today.
